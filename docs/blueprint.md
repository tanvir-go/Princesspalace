# **App Name**: SteakHouse Clone

## Core Features:

- Homepage Display: Display the layout and content of the homepage including images, text and menu sections.
- Menu Page: Present an interactive and navigable menu.
- Reservation Form: Enable users to book tables through a reservation form with date, time, and party size selection.
- Image Gallery: Showcase high-quality photos of dishes, the restaurant's interior, and ambiance.
- Contact Information: Display contact details, address, phone number, and business hours.
- Customer Reviews: Allow posting and moderation of customer reviews, as well as general site content using AI tools.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep red (#8B0000) to evoke richness and appetite.
- Background color: Off-white (#FAF9F6) to provide a clean, subtle backdrop.
- Accent color: Gold (#FFD700) to highlight special elements.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' serif for headlines, providing elegance and sophistication. Body text: 'PT Sans' sans-serif for clear, readable content.
- Use simple, elegant line icons for navigation and calls to action.
- Grid-based layout to ensure content is well-structured and easy to navigate.
- Subtle animations such as fade-ins on scroll and smooth transitions for menu items.
